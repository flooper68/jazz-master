# EPIC-003 — Progression trainer

## Goal

Drill the progressions jazz is made of — ii–V–I (major and minor), turnarounds, rhythm changes bridge — through all twelve keys, with voicing suggestions on the fretboard.

## Scope

- Progression data model: roman-numeral templates rendered into concrete keys
- Key-cycle practice: walk a progression through the cycle of fourths, random keys, or user-chosen keys
- Show suggested voicings per chord (reuses EPIC-002 library) with smooth voice-leading picks
- Configurable pace: manual advance or beat-synced advance (uses EPIC-004 metronome when available)
- Per-key mastery tracking

## Depends on

- EPIC-001 (theory core), EPIC-002 (voicings); metronome sync is optional until EPIC-004 lands

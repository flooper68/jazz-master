# EPIC-006 — Ear training

## Goal

Train the ears jazz demands: recognize chord qualities, intervals, and common progressions by sound alone.

## Scope

- Web Audio chord/interval playback (synthesized, no samples needed initially)
- Quality quiz: hear a chord → identify maj7 / m7 / 7 / m7b5 / dim7
- Interval quiz: melodic and harmonic intervals
- Progression quiz: hear ii–V–I vs I–vi–ii–V vs blues turnarounds
- Difficulty progression and accuracy tracking per category
